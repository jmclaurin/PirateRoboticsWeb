Robotic Arm Controller Instructions

Before you start controlling the arm, we've provided a list of
instructions on what action each button does. Look it over, and when you
feel comfortable enough, scroll down to begin the fun!

Left Stick

Click left/right to move
gripper to left/right, or up/down
to go backward/forward

D-Pad

Click left/right to open/close
gripper fingers, or up/down
to increase/decrease gripper spped

View Button

Menu Button

Left Bumper

Click to rotate/roll
gripper to the left

Home Button

Click for the arm to
go back to its home position

Right Bumper

Click to rotate/roll
gripper to the right

Y Button

A light turns on

B Button

Click to move gripper up/down

A Button

Click to move gripper left/right

X Button

A light turns on

Right Stick

Click left/right to rotate
gripper to left/right, or up/down
to go up/down
